About The MindHouse & Dr. Aakriti Jha

Approach to care

Dr. Jha combines evidence-based psychiatry with psychotherapy and a compassionate, collaborative style. The focus is on understanding the person in their context – home, school, work and relationships – rather than only the diagnosis.

Treatment plans are individualized and may include psycho-education, lifestyle recommendations, therapy and, where appropriate, medication – always discussed transparently with patients and families.

The MindHouse – Advanced Behavioral Clinic

The clinic offers a calm, confidential space for consultation and therapy. Special focus areas include:

Child & Adolescent

Learning difficulties, attention concerns, behavioral and emotional issues, school refusal, anxiety and mood difficulties in young people.

Women's Mental Health

Mood and anxiety concerns across the life span, perinatal mental health, stress, burnout and role-related challenges.

Psychotherapy

REBT-informed therapy and counseling for stress, anxiety, mood difficulties and interpersonal challenges in adolescents and adults.
